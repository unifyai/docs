Quaternion pose#

Collection of Pose Conversion Functions to Quaternion Pose Format

ivy_mech.pose.quaternion_pose.axis_angle_pose_to_quaternion_pose(axis_angle_pose)[source]#

Convert axis-angle pose \(\mathbf{p}_{aa} = [\mathbf{x}_c, \mathbf{e}, θ] = [x, y, z, e_x, e_y, e_z, θ]\) # noqa to quaternion pose \(\mathbf{p}_{q} = [\mathbf{x}_c, \mathbf{q}] = [x, y, z, q_i, q_j, q_k, q_r]\). # noqa

Parameters:

axis_angle_pose – Axis-angle pose [batch_shape,7]

Returns:

ret – Quaternion pose [batch_shape,7]

ivy_mech.pose.quaternion_pose.euler_pose_to_quaternion_pose(euler_pose, convention='zyx', batch_shape=None)[source]#

Convert :math: Euler angle pose \(\mathbf{p}_{abc} = [\mathbf{x}_c, \mathbf{θ}_{xyz}] = [x, y, z, ϕ_a, ϕ_b, ϕ_c]\) to quaternion pose # noqa \(\mathbf{p}_{q} = [\mathbf{x}_c, \mathbf{q}] = [x, y, z, q_i, q_j, q_k, q_r]\).n # noqa [reference] # noqa

Parameters:

  • euler_pose – Euler angle pose [batch_shape,6]

  • convention – The axes for euler rotation, in order of L.H.S. matrix multiplication. (Default value = ‘zyx’)

  • batch_shape – Shape of batch. Inferred from inputs if None. (Default value = None)

Returns:

ret – Quaternion pose [batch_shape,7]

ivy_mech.pose.quaternion_pose.increment_quaternion_pose_with_velocity(quat_pose, quat_vel, delta_t)[source]#

Increment a quaternion pose \(\mathbf{p}_{q} = [\mathbf{x}_c, \mathbf{q}] = [x, y, z, q_i, q_j, q_k, q_r]\) forward by one time-increment \(Δt\), given the velocity represented in quaternion form \(\mathbf{V}_q\). Where \(\mathbf{P}_q (t+1) = \mathbf{P}_q(t) × \mathbf{V}_q\), \(t\) is in seconds, and \(×\) denotes the hamilton product.n [reference]

Parameters:

  • quat_pose – Quaternion pose. [batch_shape,7]

  • quat_vel – Quaternion velocity. [batch_shape,7]

  • delta_t – Time step in seconds, for incrementing the quaternion pose by the velocity quaternion.

Returns:

ret – Incremented quaternion pose [batch_shape,7]

Parameters:

matrix – Matrix pose [batch_shape,3,4]

Returns:

ret – Quaternion pose [batch_shape,7]
